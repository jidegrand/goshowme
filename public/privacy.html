Privacy policy

What we see, what we don't.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

GoShowMe (goshowmeapp.com) is a live visual support tool. This page explains what data we collect when you use it, why we collect it, and what we never touch.

Who we are

GoShowMe is operated by the GoShowMe team ("we," "us"). For privacy questions, write to hi@goshowmeapp.com.

The two roles

GoShowMe has two kinds of users with very different relationships to us:

  • Tech — the support agent who creates an account and generates session links.
  • Customer — the person on the other end of the support call who clicks the link from their phone, allows the camera, and shows the issue.

Customers do not create accounts and we do not store any persistent data about them.

What the tech account stores

  • Email address (used to sign in).
  • Password hash (we never store passwords in plain text).
  • Display name, if you provide one.
  • Subscription tier and billing identifiers from our payment processor.
  • Usage counters: how many sessions you generated, against your monthly allotment.
  • Session metadata: label you gave the session, expiration time, whether it was used.

What happens during a live session

When a customer opens your link and allows their camera, the video stream is delivered peer to peer via WebRTC where possible, and through a TURN relay only when a direct path cannot be negotiated. Either way, we do not record the stream.

Audio (the voice intercom feature) follows the same path as video.

Text chat messages are relayed through our signaling server in transit. We do not store chat history once the session ends.

Plain answer

The customer's camera is never recorded by us. We don't keep video, audio, or chat from your sessions.

Vision AI

If you press a Vision AI button, the current frame is captured as a still image and sent to our AI provider (currently OpenAI) for analysis along with the system prompt for that mode and any free-form question you typed. The frame and prompt are sent through our backend to scope and rate-limit usage. We do not retain Vision AI inputs or outputs after the response is returned to your browser.

OpenAI's handling of these images is governed by their API data policy, which states that data submitted via the API is not used to train their models.

Annotations, snapshots, and freeze frames

Annotations are drawn on your tech device locally and the strokes are streamed in real time to the customer's device. Coordinates pass through our relay; the underlying camera frame they're drawn on is never copied to our servers.

Snapshots and frozen frames stay on your local computer unless you explicitly download or save them.

Cookies and tracking

We use a single first-party authentication token stored in browser local storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies on the marketing site or in the dashboard.

What we share

We share data with a small number of operational subprocessors:

  • Hosting — Railway (application hosting and websocket signaling).
  • TURN relay — Cloudflare or Metered, used only when peer-to-peer cannot be negotiated.
  • Vision AI — OpenAI, used only when you trigger a Vision AI action.
  • Payments — our payment processor for paid plans (data limited to billing).

We do not sell user data.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your account data, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete your account by writing to hi@goshowmeapp.com. We will respond within 30 days. Customers (the people clicking session links) are anonymous to us and have no account to delete; the act of ending the session deletes everything we knew about them.

Children

GoShowMe is not intended for use by children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data about them.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will update the date at the top and notify signed-in techs by email. Continued use after a change constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Privacy questions: hi@goshowmeapp.com.
