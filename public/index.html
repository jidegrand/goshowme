The problem

Traditional remote support
breaks when you need it most.

Every existing tool assumes the user's computer is working. When it isn't, you're flying blind on a phone call.

PC is too broken to install
TeamViewer, AnyDesk, and RDP all require a working OS and admin rights. Exactly what you don't have in a real emergency.
Phone calls are blind
"Bottom left corner" — "I don't see it." 40 minutes of this. No visual context means guesswork, not support.
Screen share needs a working screen
BSOD, blank display, resolution issues — if the screen is the problem, screen sharing is useless by definition.
Video calls require setup
Zoom, Teams, Google Meet — all need accounts, downloads, and enough tech literacy to set them up. Which is exactly why they called you.

Features

Everything a support pro needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built to be the fastest tool in your stack — not the most complex.

One-time expiring links
Each session URL is single-use with a configurable TTL — 15 min to 2 hours. Expired links show a clean end screen, not a 404.
Peer-to-peer stream
WebRTC direct connection. Video never touches our servers. No recording, no storage, no liability — unless you explicitly enable it.
SMS & email share
One click to send the session link via SMS or pre-filled email directly from your dashboard. Copy-to-clipboard fallback always available.
Snapshot capture
Freeze a frame from the live feed with one click. Saves locally as a PNG — perfect for attaching to tickets or documenting the issue.
Full consent screen
Every user sees who is watching, for how long, and that it's not recorded. Clear, transparent, legally sound from day one.
Automatic TTL expiry
Sessions expire on their own. No open camera links floating around. Tokens are single-use and destroyed on session end or timeout.
