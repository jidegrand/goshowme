The problem

Support calls need more than screen share.
They need sight, context, and guidance.

GoShowMe is built for the messy moments: dead monitors, loose cables, BIOS screens, blinking LEDs, error dialogs, and users who should not have to install anything.

The computer may be unusable
Remote desktop tools still need a working OS, network, permissions, and user confidence. GoShowMe only needs the user's phone browser.
Phone calls lose visual context
When you cannot see the screen, cable, port, or LED, every instruction gets slower. A live camera feed turns guesswork into directed support.
Screen share misses the physical world
The issue might be a power brick, ethernet jack, drive bay, router light, barcode, or a laptop hinge. Screen sharing cannot inspect hardware.
Video meetings add friction
Meeting apps still ask the user to pick cameras, join rooms, find buttons, and explain what they see. GoShowMe opens directly into a consent-first camera session.

FAQ

Answers before sign-up.

Does the customer have to install anything?+
No. The customer opens the link in their phone's browser, taps Allow camera, and the live feed starts. Works on iOS Safari and Android Chrome out of the box.
Is the camera ever recorded?+
No. Video and audio travel peer-to-peer between the two browsers using DTLS-SRTP encryption. We don't record, persist, or post-process the stream. Snapshots and frozen frames you create stay on your computer unless you export them. Full posture is on the security page.
What does the customer see when they click the link?+
A clear consent screen showing who's asking, that the camera will be shared, and how long the link is valid. They tap once to allow camera access and the support session starts. They can end the session at any time from their phone.
Which devices and browsers are supported?+
Customer: any modern phone browser — iOS 14.5+ Safari, Android 9+ Chrome, recent Edge or Firefox on mobile. Tech: any desktop browser with WebRTC support — Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari. No app downloads, ever.
How accurate is the Vision AI?+
Vision AI is a useful tier-1 assistant for reading screen text, identifying ports and cables, and suggesting next steps. It is generated by a model and can be wrong. Treat its output as a hint to verify, not a final answer. The frame is sent to OpenAI for analysis only when you press an AI button; we don't retain inputs or outputs.
Does it work over a weak connection?+
Yes. WebRTC adapts the bitrate down for low bandwidth. If a direct path can't be negotiated (CGNAT, strict firewall), the stream falls back to a TURN relay automatically so the call still works. Voice keeps flowing even when video gets choppy.
Can I send the link by SMS or email?+
Yes. Generate the session link from the dashboard and send it however you talk to your customer — SMS, email, WhatsApp, Slack, or a copy-paste into your ticketing tool. The link expires on the schedule you chose.
How does pricing and cancellation work?+
The Starter plan is free forever with 5 sessions per month. Pro is $19 per tech seat per month, billed monthly. Team is $49 for up to 5 seats. Cancel any time from your dashboard — no exit fees, no calls. The plan stays active until the end of the billing period you already paid for.
